Why does the HBO-ICT program suit me?

Since I've always been interested in computers and technology, I believe the HBO-ICT curriculum is a good fit for me. I appreciate making my own projects and learning out how things operate. In addition to understanding how systems work, being able to master both the theoretical and practical parts of ICT enables me to actually create something with them, which is something I really appreciate about it. Furthermore, I understand that ICT is continually changing, therefore I will never become tired and will always be learning new things.

I'm particularly interested in the following areas of ICT:

  • I'm interested in game design because I've always liked playing games and I want to know how they're developed. Not only do i just like games i like problem solving wich is involved in coding.
  • Software development, because I like creating programs and seeing my own ideas become reality. I enjoy seeing results from your work, it makes me feel proud of what i did and happy with what i acomplished.
  • Investigating different ICT fields to see what else I might find enjoyable in the future. Im interested in exploring and finding new things i enjoy this gives me new stuff to think about or do wich is always good.

HBO-ICT is the best option for me since it combines my interests with practical skills that will be valuable in the future.
