Frequently asked questions

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

First you need to go to this page, log into your account and after that you can upload all the files you want to print. After choosing the file you can set up a couple more things and confirm your request. Next, you go to the printer (for example on the first floor next to the staircase), scan your card, choose printing option and your documents should be printed.

How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

Scanning to a personal/external mailbox is not possible. Please do not change the scanner settings. Log in to the printer using your HZ card or by entering your HZ username and password then select SCAN from the menu. Place the originals on the feeder or scanner glass. For double-sided scanning, press Change settings, enable 2-sided scanning in duplex mode, then press Start. After scanning, press the icon at the top right to log out.

How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?

Go to the webshop page, then add the items you want to your cart and pay.

How can you book a project space?

To book a project space, go to this site. Then click on “Book,” go to “Book a meeting or project room,” and reserve a space!

What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

You need to show your HZ pass at the barriers. After that, you can park in the lot.
