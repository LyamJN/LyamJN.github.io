Company Exploration

Mojang Studios is a company i find quite inspiring, they created one of the most sold games of all time, Minecraft. I personally love Minecraft. Minecraft was started bt one single person and over-time became a HUGE game in the industry, seeing how such a big game can come from one person is the part i find so inspiring. Mojang who later bought Minecraft really tries to keep that origin in their game, knowing it came from one person and they try to keep it that style. Mojang has around 700 staff members wich i find quite impressive when their games are massive in scale. They have allot of different jobs some examples are software developers and engineers for Java and C++ but also data analysts or marketing positions. I think it would be fun to work at Mojang Studios but challenging. Not only do they only have 2 places where they work from but getting to a level that high requires allot of skill.
