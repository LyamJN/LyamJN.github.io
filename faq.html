Frequently asked questions.

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

First you need to go to this page, log into your account and after that you can upload all the files you want to print. After choosing file you can set up a couple of more things and confirm your request. Next, you go to the printer (for example on the first floor next to the staircase), scan your card, choose printing option and your documents should be printed.

How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

Scanning to a personal/external mailbox is not possible. Please do not change the scanner settings. Log in to the printer using your HZ card or by entering your HZ username and password them select SCAN from the menu, Place the original(s) on the feeder or scanner glass. Double-sided scanning? (optional), Press Change settings, Enable 2-sided scanning in the duplex mode then press Start, After scanning, press on the icon at the top right to log out.

How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?

First go to the webshop pagethen add the items you want to your cart and pay.

How can you book a project space?

To book a project space go to this site. Then click on to book afterwards go to book a meeting or project room and reserve a space!

What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

You need to reveal you HZ pass at the barriers. After that you can park in the lot.
